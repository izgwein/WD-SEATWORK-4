ABOUT US

Welcome to Hello, Future! A heartfelt creation by someone who believes in the power of dreams and the beauty of hope. Our founder, a sentimental soul, created this service as a way to find reasons to look forward to the future. In a world that often feels uncertain and overwhelming, Bubbles of Tomorrow offers a gentle reminder that our dreams and aspirations can guide us through.

Our Story

Hello, Future was born from a personal journey of seeking hope and meaning. Our founder, a person with a tender heart and a love for the poetic, discovered solace in the idea of sending messages to their future self. This simple yet profound act became a source of comfort and inspiration, sparking the idea to share this experience with others who might be feeling the same way.
